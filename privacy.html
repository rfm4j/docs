Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Focus Room ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of our application.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use Focus Room.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your usage, location, or behavioral data.
  • Local Storage: All your data (including room settings, timers, and preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and file system. We do not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

Focus Room does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that collect user data.
